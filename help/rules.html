Rules

The object of the game is to remove all 144 tiles. A tile may only be removed if:

  • it does not have any tiles on top of it;
  • there are no tiles either to its left or right, or both;
  • it matches another tile which meets the above criteria (i.e. it is open).

Two tiles match if they are of the same suit and rank, except for season and flower tiles, which match any tile of the same suit.

A fireworks display is shown after all tiles have been removed.
